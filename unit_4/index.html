The HallPass Experience

Everyone should have the opportunity to pursue their dreams. We strive to connect students to companies they want to work for, and give them the opportunity to take courses at a piece-meal basis, with the goal of gaining the neccessary skills for the positions they are interested in pursuing. This is the higher education of the future.

Brand Story

At HallPass, we are passionate about helping people reach their career goals. We want to make this process as simple and straightforward as possible. Our brand is clean and sharp, with the purpose of facilitating the use of our service.
