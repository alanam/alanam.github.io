Brand Story

At HallPass, we are passionate about helping people reach their career goals. We want to make this process as simple and straightforward as possible. Our brand is clean and sharp, with the purpose of facilitating the use of our service.

The HallPass Experience

Everyone should have the opportunity to pursue their dreams. However, for some, higher education can often times seem like an unreachable and expensive goal. We believe that there is an alternative to the traditional four year degree. We strive to connect students to companies they want to work for, and give them the opportunity to take courses at a piece-meal basis, with the goal of gaining the neccessary skills for the positions they are interested in pursuing. This model will allow someone who otherwise would not pursue higher education to gain the necessary skills to land great jobs. We believe this is the higher education of the future.

Brand Identity

A brand is more than words and a logo. It is a philosophical approach to how a company expresses itself and the story it tells. Brands who understand themselves completely have better overall communication. That is why we don not try to be all things to everyone. Rather, we focus on being ourselves, true to our core purpose and what makes us unique in the world. A consistent voice and tone, combined with visuals that express our brand story, help us to express this through a cohesive, recognizable brand personality.
